A little about us.

So. you are an adventure seeker!!

Kooks adventures is born out of a deep wish to preserve a raw, earthy space close to nature amidst the growing bustle of urban life. Though our guests may come to kooks for brief sojourn, the kind of bond and community that we rejuvenate here with nature is certainly not ephemeral. As a company, Kooks seeks to provide the best of experiences by thorough organisation of our activities. And yet, the tryst that we promise our guests with nature's invigorating spontaneity is never interrupted by anything too mechanical or interfered by anything plastic. We wish that you remember kooks adventures not just one of many tourist getaways, but also as a space etched in your memory that will forever be a source of calm.

Forest Hiking

Forest trekking at its best. No trip in to the beauty of wayanad gets complete without the adventure of hiking! into the woods and through the captivating foliage, we take you to two enchanting spots, that will suerly enthrall your senses. One is a fascinating view of the picteresque mountain range while the other is a beautiful waterfall.

Tent/Room stay

With so much of the jungle happening around you, why sleep indoors? We have got tentstays with tents of the best brands in amazing, secure locations for those who truly want to be one with the forest. Take home the enhancing experience of crickets and cicads lulling you to sleep and waking up to the humming and chirping of the forest coming alive around you!
